Our mission

Young volunteers creating practical kindness.

Founded in 2021, Music Angels Hands teaches students the value of community involvement, active volunteer service, and the power of helping others. Members plan projects, host drives, share music, and grow into leaders by doing the work together.

Mission/Vision

Young musicians serving with practical compassion.

Our mission is to help students use music, time, and teamwork to support children and families who need encouragement, resources, and community.

Our vision is a student-led network where every member can grow as a volunteer, communicator, organizer, and compassionate leader.
