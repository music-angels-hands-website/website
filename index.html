Student-led service through music

Changing the lives of children with compassion, creativity, and action.

Music Angels Hands brings students together to serve local and global communities, support underserved children, and learn leadership by building projects from idea to impact.

Our mission

Young volunteers creating practical kindness.

Founded in 2021, Music Angels Hands teaches students the value of community involvement, active volunteer service, and the power of helping others. Members plan projects, host drives, share music, and grow into leaders by doing the work together.
