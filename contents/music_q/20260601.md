# SHORT FILM REFLECTION

Pixar Short Films #7

For the Birds (2000)

*Submitted by Yewon Seong*

**1. Why was the yellow umbrella important to Joseph?**

A: I think the yellow umbrella was important to Joseph because it made him feel comfortable and like he belonged. He cared a lot about being accepted by the other birds, so he followed what the group was doing instead of treating the bigger bird kindly.

**2. What emotions did you feel?**

A: I felt entertained because the film was funny, but I also felt bad for the larger bird because the other birds were making fun of him. The ending made me feel satisfied because the rude birds got a consequence for their actions.

**3. What message did the filmmakers want to share?**

A: I think the filmmakers wanted viewers to learn the people should not judge or make fun of others for being different.
